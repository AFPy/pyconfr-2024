Schedule

October 31th & November 1st

Participative Coding: sprints!

Developers of various open source projects gather in little groups to code together. Everyone is welcome to contribute, and we seek to guide beginners.

Take part to the development of your favorite project!

We provide the space and environment (tables, chairs, internet connection… and coffee!).

November 2nd & 3rd

Conferences and Workshops

During these two days, you will have the opportunity to attend to presentations on various subjects, about the Python programming language:

  • use cases
  • good practices
  • feedbacks
  • shared ideas

Registration

PyConFR's sprints, talks and workshops are free.

However, registration is mandatory for everyone, including organization staff, volunteers, speakers and sponsors.

Registration is open until the end of the event, limited to:

  • 200 places for sprints,
  • 1 000 places for talks and workshops,
  • 100 places for the Saturday night dinner.
PyConFR Registration

With this registration, it is possible to:

  • reserve t-shirts (€15), also sold during the event with limited stocks,
  • reserve a place for the Saturday night dinner (€30).

⚠️ Ordering t-shirts and booking the Saturday evening party will incur a fee for AFPy. No refunds will be possible after the event.

Registration for the children's area is open until 15 days before the start of the event, i.e. October 15.

You can also support AFPy, the association organizing PyConFR, with a donation. If you want to vote during its general assembly on Sunday morning, you must become a member.

Children Area Registration

Children's Area

A children's area is set up during the four days of the PyConFR and is hosted by Babychou Services.

The children's area accommodates a maximum of 18 children, aged 3 to 12 years.

Registration will be open at the same time as event registration and will remain open until 15 days before the start of PyConFR. Priority is given to people running sprints, conferences and workshops.

Saturday night dinner

A dinner is organized on Saturday night, from 7:30 PM to 11:00 PM, only for the ones who chose the option during their registration.

This dinner includes a cocktail buffet with petit fours and soft and alcoholic drinks.

Other alcoholic drinks can be bought (not included in the price) during the dinner.

⚠️ The meal does not include a vegan option ⚠️

The menu will be available soon.
