Reach the Campus

By car

When approaching Strasbourg take the direction of Kehl bypassing the city center by the south (quartier Neudorf) then follow porte des Schirmeck (Exit 4 of A35).

You come by car and you have room? Consider carsharing :).

Public transport

The University is reachable by public transport:

  • By tram: stop Université on lines C, E and F,
  • By tram: stop Esplanade on lines C and F,
  • By bus: stop Cité Administrative on lines 30 and C1.

From the train-station (approx. 12 minutes)

From the Central Train-station of Strasbourg, you can take the C tram to Université stop.

From the aeroport (approx. 8 minutes)

Strasbourg airport is located at Entzheim. A train (TER) allow you to read the central railway station in 8 minutes 5 times per hour.

Self-Service Bikes

You can also reach the campus using self-service bikes (called "vélhop"). The closest station is Université.

Housing Advice

Try to find an accomodation close to the C, E or F lines of Strasbourg's tramway, the fastest way to reach the university.

  • Esplanade Hotel (~70€ per night) — 5 minutes walk
  • Patricia Hotel(~60€ per night) — 20 minutes walk
  • Ibis Budget centre gare (~90-100€ per night) — 15 minutes by tram or 30 minutes walk
  • City Residence Access (~50€ per night) — 20 minutes by public transports or 30 minutes walk
  • F1 Pont de l'Europe (~40€ per night) — 20 minutes by public transports

Note that you will also find hotel areas outside the city (Illkirch, Schiltigheim, Kehl).
